# 🚀 AI Sales Copilot

AI Sales Copilot is an AI-powered B2B sales assistant that helps businesses **find leads, enrich company data, and generate personalized outreach messages automatically**.  
This tool simplifies the lead generation and sales outreach process by combining **Lead Discovery, AI-powered messaging, and CRM-style lead management** in one platform.

---

## 📌 Features

### 🔎 AI Lead Finder
Search and discover potential B2B leads using filters like:
- Industry
- Company size
- Location
- Job title

Displays a list of companies and decision makers.

---

### 🧠 AI Lead Enrichment
Automatically enriches lead data with:
- Company description
- Website
- Industry
- Estimated employee size
- Social media links
- AI-generated company insights

---

### ✉️ AI SDR (Sales Development Representative)
Generate personalized outreach messages using AI:

- Cold Emails
- LinkedIn Messages
- Follow-up email sequences
